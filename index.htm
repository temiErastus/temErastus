WELCOME TO ATLANTIC MAPS

Map Atlantic is a cartographic firm that uses modern technology to create beautifully designed maps. We produce both web and print maps using GIS technologies such as QGIS, ArcGIS, Mapbox with scripting languages (javascript and python) and MySQL for the database. We conduct wide research in order not to miss any neccessary detail. We do things from data management, data creation, analysis, graphics, layout until your product is ready.

Clients from different fields use our services for recreation maps, municipal planning, research and much more.

OUR SERVICES INCLUDE

Web and Print Maps

We design and produce maps with out missing much details for your website or any purpose you choose. Printed maps that suits your purpose is also available in any format (A3, A4 and more).

GIS Consulting

We will help you collect, analyze, store and manipulate spatial data. Our work can e used in a variety of fields. Our analysis can be used to find research and data by mapping the spread of disease, plan for urban and rural forestry, find suitable building locations and analyse sustainability.

Research and Reference Maps

We produce maps for research purpose. We can help you handle the geographical data for your project. We produce ondemand maps.

Urban and Municipal Planning

We digitize excellent layouts of urban and rural areas for planning by government agencies, real estate companies, waste managenet agencies and more generally organizations that has environmental protection duties.

Training Facilitators and e-learning

We facilitate both online and offline training for the use of tools on themes ranging from map production, GIS, remote sensing and web map with web development.
